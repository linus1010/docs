## âœ… Step 3: Create a Service Account

1. Visit: [Service Accounts](https://console.cloud.google.com/iam-admin/serviceaccounts)
2. Click **+ CREATE SERVICE ACCOUNT**
3. Name it:
   `configview-sa`
4. Click **Create and Continue**
5. (Optional) Skip assigning roles
6. Click **Done**
7. Open the service account > go to **Details** > click **Edit**
8. Enable **G Suite Domain-wide Delegation**
9. Set product name to: `ConfigView App`
10. Click **Save**
