## ‚úÖ Step 2: Enable Required APIs

In the `configview-integration` project:

1. Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis
   Click **Enable API's and services**.
2. Enable the following APIs by searching for them and clicking on the links below:
   - **Gmail API**: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/library/gmail.googleapis.com
   - **Admin SDK**: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/library/admin.googleapis.com
   - **Groups Settings**: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/library/groupssettings.googleapis.com

---

## ‚úÖ Step 3: Create a Service Account and JSON Key

 1. Visit: [Service Accounts](https://console.cloud.google.com/iam-admin/serviceaccounts)
 2. Click **\+ CREATE SERVICE ACCOUNT**
 3. Name it:
    ¬† ¬†`configview-sa`
    _Note: The Service Account ID generated will also need to match this name._
 4. Click **Create and Continue**
 5. (Optional) Skip assigning roles
 6. Click **Done**
 7. Open the service account \> Click **Keys** \> click **Add Key** \> **Create new key** \> choose **Json** \> click **Create**.
 8. Go back to **Details** \> Click **Advanced Settings**.
 9. Set product name to: `ConfigView App`
10. Enable **G Suite Domain-wide Delegation**.
11. Click **Save**.

2. Click **Advanced Settings**.
3. **Copy the Client ID**: This will be a long string of numbers.

5. In the Google Admin console, navigate to:
