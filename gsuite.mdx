## âœ… Step 1: Create a Google Cloud Project

1. Go to: [https://console.cloud.google.com/](https://console.cloud.google.com/)
2. Click the **project dropdown** (top-left) > **New Project**
3. **Recommended Project Name:**  
   `configview-integration`
4. Choose your **Organization** and **Location**
5. Click **Create**
6. Select the project from the dropdown

---

## âœ… Step 2: Enable Required APIs

In the `configview-integration` project:

1. Go to: [API Library](https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/library)
2. Enable the following APIs:
   - **Gmail API**
   - **Admin SDK**
   - **Groups Settings API**

---

## âœ… Step 3: Create a Service Account

1. Visit: [Service Accounts](https://console.cloud.google.com/iam-admin/serviceaccounts)
2. Click **+ CREATE SERVICE ACCOUNT**
3. Name it:  
   `configview-sa`
4. Click **Create and Continue**
5. (Optional) Skip assigning roles
6. Click **Done**
7. Open the service account > go to **Details** > click **Edit**
8. Enable **G Suite Domain-wide Delegation**
9. Set product name to: `ConfigView App`
10. Click **Save**

---
