## ✅ Step 1: Create a Google Cloud Project

> 💳 **Note:** Google requires you to set up a **billing account** to use projects.
> If you don’t have one, you’ll need to create it before proceeding.
> **This ConfigView integration does not incur any cost in Google Cloud.**
> If you already have a billing account, simply choose your **Organization** and **Location** during project creation.

1. Go to: [https://console.cloud.google.com/](https://console.cloud.com/)
2. Click the **project dropdown** (top-left) \> **New Project**
3. **Recommended Project Name:**
      `configview-integration`
4. Choose your **Organization** and **Location**
5. Click **Create**
6. Select the project from the dropdown

---

## ✅ Step 2: Enable Required APIs

In the `configview-integration` project:

1. Go to: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis
   Click **Enable API's and services**.
2. Enable the following APIs by searching for them and clicking on the links below:
   - **Gmail API**: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/library/gmail.googleapis.com
   - **Admin SDK**: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/library/admin.googleapis.com
   - **Groups Settings**: https://console.cloud.google.com/apis/library/groupssettings.googleapis.com

---

## ✅ Step 3: Create a Service Account and JSON Key

 1. Visit: [Service Accounts](https://console.cloud.google.com/iam-admin/serviceaccounts)
 2. Click **\+ CREATE SERVICE ACCOUNT**
 3. Name it:
       `configview-sa`
    _Note: The Service Account ID generated will also need to match this name._
 4. Click **Create and Continue**
 5. (Optional) Skip assigning roles
 6. Click **Done**
 7. Open the service account \> Click **Keys** \> click **Add Key** \> **Create new key** \> choose **Json** \> click **Create**.
 8. Go back to **Details** \> Click **Advanced Settings**.
 9. Set product name to: `ConfigView App`
10. Enable **G Suite Domain-wide Delegation**.
11. Click **Save**.

---

## ✅ Step 4: Rename JSON Key

1. A key file was downloaded in the previous step.
2. 🔁 **Rename the file to:**
      `google_admin.json`
      _This is required for ConfigView to authenticate with Google Workspace._

---

## ✅ Step 5: Authorize Domain-Wide Delegation in Google Admin

1. Go to the service account **Details** tab in Google Cloud Console.
2. Click **Advanced Settings**.
3. **Copy the Client ID**: This will be a long string of numbers.
4. Click the link: **"View Google Workspace admin console"** to open the Google Admin console in a new tab.
5. In the Google Admin console, navigate to:

   \* **Security** \> **Access and Data Control** \> **API Controls**
6. Scroll to **Domain-wide Delegation** \> click **Manage Domain-Wide Delegation**
7. Click **Add New**
8. Paste the **Client ID** you copied earlier.
